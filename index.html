FAQ

A non-fungible token (NFT) is a unique digital asset that can cryptographically assign and prove ownership of digital goods. As a collector you can buy, own, trade and enjoy the possibility of NFTs.

Owning Cryptostal will grant you access to future events with the expansion of the community.

To buy an NFT you need to have a wallet with enough ETH and a Metamask or any other common plug-in, everything goes on OpenSea platform for Cryptostal project, you can watch this video of OpenSea for a quick grasp: https://opensea.io/#meetopensea

We also recommend first-time buyers do their own research before purchase any NFTs.
